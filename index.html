Crystal Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game. Your score will initially equal the random number.


There are 4 crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will deduct a specific amount of points from your score.


You win the game by reducing your total score to zero, you lose the game if your total score goes below zero.


The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will randomly change the value of each crystal.
